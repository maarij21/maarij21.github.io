ABOUT ME

Hello world! I'm Maarij, a hardworking student at McMaster University, currently in my 3rd year studying Computer Science.

As a kid, I grew up spending summer breaks at my mom's school, where she taught computer science to children aged 7-15. Here I was introduced to the world of computer science and it instantly sparked my curiosity. My journey began from creating small games to writing more complex algorithms to solve problems that felt more rewarding. I quickly realized that this is where my passions lie. After completing highschool in Dubai, UAE, I moved to Hamilton, Canada to pursue this passion further.

Through projects, assignments and various courses, I have learnt a ton and gained experience in languages such as Python, Java, SQL, Bash, Haskell, Javascript and HTML/CSS as well as worked with frameworks such as Django, Git and React. My current career goals are to gain exposure and firsthand experience in industry as a software developer intern so I can learn from talented, like-minded individuals and the ability to apply my skills in bigger projects. I am in a dedicated co-op program and open to exploring new opportunities!
